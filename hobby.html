MY HOBBY: PLAYING GAMES

My favorite hobby is playing games. It helps me relax and enjoy my free time while keeping my mind active. I like to challenge myself by trying new strategies. As the saying goes: Games give you a chance to excel, and if you’re playing in good company, you don’t even mind if you lose. — Gary Gygax

I enjoy both online and offline games. Sometimes I play simple mobile games, and other times I dive into action-packed PC games. My excitement level goes up to 100% whenever I win, and drops to almost 0% when I lose — but that’s all part of the fun!
